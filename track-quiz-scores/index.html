Do you really need an LMS?

Most people only use only five of the hundreds of features most learning management systems have. They just want to know that someone took a quiz, what they got on it, and when. On top of that, they want simple reports that are easy to pull. Well, Qzzr isn’t an LMS, but it does all of those things.

Create a graded quiz in just a few minutes

We created Qzzr to be the world’s simplest quiz tool. Creating an engaging, beautiful quiz is actually quite fun and can be done in a snap.

Share it with your people

Whether you’re a teacher, trainer, professor, or manager, you can share your quiz in a myriad of ways. Send them an email. Embed it on a site. Drop a link in a slide deck. Send them to your profile page. Boom!

Track scores

When people take your quiz for credit, you will be able to track who took your quiz, when, and what their score is. You can export this data to any other system your little heart desires.

Take quiz on any device

Qzzr works flawlessly and looks beautiful on any screen or device. It also means your quiz takers will never have to download an app or software. Qzzr just works.
