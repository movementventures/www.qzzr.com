Engage your visitors

Quizzes offer a low risk, high reward way for a visitor to engage with your brand. Instead of creating a quiz of fluff, ask them serious questions about their needs and how they map to your ability to fill them and you have a powerful tool on your hands.

Help them decide

Under the hood of an outcome quiz is really a decision engine. Based on preferences, you can make a recommendation to a visitor... just like an expert would. How much more likely are your customers to buy after they speak with an expert?

Provide an education

Alternatively to an outcome quiz, you could create a graded quiz with great feedback that teaches your visitors about your product, brand, or service. Typically our quizzes have completion rates of over 80% which means they are holding a visitors attention for 3-5 minutes.

Customize your offer

Once you create a compelling offer and description, you can customize the button label and set the URL it directs to. You can also add any tracking codes to that URL that you desire.

Track conversion rates

In the quiz analytics, you can measure the funnel from visitors to offers in one quick glance.
